What We Do

More than chess.
A development system.

We combine a smart chess platform, physical smart boards, and a cognitive assessment engine to generate measurable developmental data — automatically, from every session your students play.

♟
Adaptive Chess Engine
Five adaptive difficulty levels from beginner to near-master. Elo rating system, live multiplayer, tactical puzzles, and opening academy — all in one place.
🧠
Cognitive Assessment
A 48-question baseline test across 5 cognitive domains — working memory, pattern recognition, processing speed, reasoning, and inhibitory control. Repeated every 90 days.
📊
Behavioral Tracking
Every game generates data on impulse control, resilience, emotional regulation, focus, and completion rate — automatically, with no teacher input required.
🎓
Teacher Dashboard
See every student's chess rating, puzzle accuracy, cognitive scores, and behavioral metrics in one place. Know who needs support before problems escalate.
💡
LED Smart Boards
3D-printed, Hall sensor-equipped chess boards that light up with legal moves, last moves, and engine suggestions — and sync every physical move to the platform in real time.
👨‍👩‍👧
Parent Portal
Families see their child's chess progress, cognitive scores, and behavioral metrics from home — keeping the whole family engaged in their child's development.

Our Story

Born from necessity.
Built with purpose.

Our co-founder grew up in Brooklyn, New York — an under-resourced, at-risk community where school funding gaps left critical developmental needs unmet. The school system she was part of wasn't built to serve children like her.

Then chess found her. An after-school chess program gave her something her school couldn't: structure, cognitive challenge, focused mentorship, and a reason to keep showing up. The game demanded that she think ahead, regulate her emotions under pressure, and persist through failure — skills the classroom never taught her.

Chess opened doors to scholarships. Scholarships opened doors to NYU. NYU opened doors to the world — despite every systemic barrier stacked against her from birth.

The Classical Chess Collegium was built so every child has access to that same transformative experience — regardless of zip code, household income, or school budget. We believe chess is not just a game. It is a cognitive development system hiding in plain sight, and we intend to make it available to every community that needs it.

The Beginning
Brooklyn, New York. At-risk community. Underfunded school. Critical developmental gaps no one was filling.
The Turning Point
An after-school chess program provides the structure, cognitive challenge, and mentorship the school system couldn't offer.
The Pathway Opens
Chess scholarships emerge as a pathway out — one that would have been impossible without the program.
The Goal
NYU. Graduated against the odds. Every systemic barrier cleared. The game changed everything.
Today
Building The Classical Chess Collegium — the platform she needed as a child, now available to every school that will have us.
